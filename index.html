Project NYR

Independent Game Studio

Project NYR is an independent studio building games and interactive experiences for thinking beings. We believe the best games are the ones that reward curiosity — games that are easy to start, but reveal new depth the longer you play.

Word Arc is our first title: a game born from the conviction that word games and puzzle games belong together. Every mechanic, every language pack, every story card is designed to create moments of genuine discovery.

We are building slowly and carefully — because the games worth playing are the ones worth making properly.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Project NYR ("we", "us") operates the Word Arc mobile application. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

Word Arc does not directly collect personal information. However, the following third-party services integrated into the app may collect data to provide their services:

  • Google AdMob — serves advertisements and may collect device identifiers and usage data.
  • Unity LevelPlay (IronSource) — ad mediation platform that collects advertising-related data.
  • Firebase Analytics — collects anonymised usage events and crash reports to help us improve the app.
  • Firebase Crashlytics — collects crash data and device information for stability improvements.

Advertising & Consent

Word Arc is ad-supported. On first launch, you will be asked for consent to personalised advertising in accordance with GDPR (EEA users) and applicable regional privacy laws. You may opt out of personalised ads at any time through your device settings.

Children

Word Arc is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Retention & Deletion

We do not store personal data on our own servers. For data collected by third-party services, please refer to their respective privacy policies: Google / AdMob, IronSource, Firebase.

Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at hello@projectnyr.com.
