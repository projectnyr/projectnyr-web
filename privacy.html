Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

Overview

Project NYR ("we", "us", "our") operates the Word Arc mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and shared when you use our App.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. Word Arc does not directly collect or store personal information on our own servers.

Information Collected by Third-Party Services

The following third-party services are integrated into Word Arc and may collect data to provide their services. Each service operates under its own privacy policy:

  • Google AdMob — serves advertisements within the App. May collect device identifiers, IP address, and usage data for ad targeting and measurement. Google Privacy Policy →
  • Unity LevelPlay (IronSource) — ad mediation platform that manages ad delivery. Collects advertising-related identifiers and interaction data. IronSource Privacy Policy →
  • Firebase Analytics — collects anonymised in-app usage events to help us understand how players interact with the App and improve the experience. Firebase Privacy Policy →
  • Firebase Crashlytics — collects crash reports and device information to help us identify and fix stability issues. Firebase Privacy Policy →

Advertising & Consent (GDPR / CCPA)

Word Arc is ad-supported. On first launch, users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and other applicable regions will be presented with a consent request in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable regional privacy laws.

You may choose to receive personalised or non-personalised advertisements. You can update your consent preferences or opt out of personalised ads at any time through your device settings:

  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID or opt out of ads personalisation

Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Word Arc is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided personal information through our App, please contact us and we will take steps to remove such information.

Data Retention & Deletion

We do not store personal data on our own servers. All data collection is performed by the third-party services listed above. For data retention periods and deletion requests, please refer to the respective privacy policies of each service.

To request deletion of data associated with your device, you may reset your advertising ID through your device settings or contact the relevant third-party service directly.

Data Deletion Request

To request deletion of any data associated with your use of Word Arc, please contact us by email. We will process your request and, where applicable, coordinate with our third-party service providers to action the deletion.

hello@projectnyr.com — Subject: Data Deletion Request

Please include the following in your email:

  • The device type and operating system you used
  • Your advertising ID (Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Your advertising ID)
  • A description of the data you would like deleted

We will respond within 30 days. Note that data held by third-party services (AdMob, Firebase, IronSource) must be requested directly from those providers using the links in the section above.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
